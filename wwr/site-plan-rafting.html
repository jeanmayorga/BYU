Overview

Purpose

To provide an engaging and informative online platform for individuals and groups interested in experiencing the thrill and excitement of rafting in the beautiful natural landscapes of BYU and its surrounding areas, while also promoting safety, environmental responsibility, and building a community of like-minded adventure seekers.

Audience

Our target audience is individuals and groups, primarily between the ages of 18-45, who are interested in outdoor recreational activities and seeking adventure and thrills in their lives. They may have a moderate to high level of fitness, enjoy nature, and have a disposable income to spend on outdoor experiences. They are likely to be students, professionals, and families living in or visiting the Provo/Orem area. They want to have a unique and memorable experience while also learning about safety and environmental responsibility. They may not be aware of the various options available to them for rafting in the BYU area. They will typically access our site from their desktop or mobile device while researching and planning their outdoor activities.
